## What is Snus?

Swedish snus is a moist, smokeless tobacco product placed under the upper lip (between gum and lip). Unlike American dipping tobacco, Swedish snus is **steam-pasteurized** (not fermented), resulting in lower levels of tobacco-specific nitrosamines (TSNAs). It is deeply embedded in Swedish culture.

**Usage:** Place a portion or pinch under the upper lip. Hold 15–60 minutes. Nicotine is absorbed through the oral mucosa. No chewing, no spitting.

**History:** Moist oral snus emerged in the early 1800s. Ettan — one of the oldest continuously produced brands — was created in 1822. Swedish Match (now owned by Philip Morris International since 2022) became the dominant manufacturer.

## Formats

### Lös (Loose)
- Traditional form: loose moist tobacco manually formed into a "prilla" (cylinder/ball) by the user
- More control over portion size, direct nicotine hit
- Usually sold in larger tins (50g)

### Portion snus (portionssnus)
Pre-portioned pouches in a sachet. Dominant format today.

| Sub-type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Original / Brun** | Pre-moistened, brown pouch. Fast flavor/nicotine, slight drip |
| **White (Vit)** | Drier outer pouch. Less drip, slower/longer flavor release |
| **White Dry** | Even drier than white. Minimal drip |
| **Slim** | Narrow, discreet profile. Very popular modern format |
| **Mini** | Smallest format. Very discreet, lower total nicotine per pouch |
| **Large / Maxi** | Larger pouch. More tobacco and nicotine per portion |

### Nikotinpåsar / All White (Tobacco-free)
- No tobacco leaf. White pouches with nicotine salts, plant fiber, flavorings
- Brands: ZYN, Velo, Lyft, White Fox, XQS, Klint, Skruf Super White
- No brown staining, completely white during use

## Nicotine Levels

Nicotine is measured in **mg per gram of product (mg/g)**.

| Category | Swedish | Typical mg/g |
|---|---|---|
| Normal | Normal | 6–10 mg/g |
| Strong | Stark | 11–14 mg/g |
| Extra Strong | Extra Stark | 15–20 mg/g |
| Extreme | Extreme | 20+ mg/g |

**Per-portion nicotine** = mg/g × gram weight of portion
Example: 1g portion at 8 mg/g = ~8 mg nicotine total

Typical portion weights: mini ~0.5g, slim ~0.9g, regular ~1g, large ~1.5g

**Notable examples:**
- General White Original: ~8 mg/g
- General Extra Stark: ~14 mg/g
- Knox Original: ~8 mg/g
- Knox Stark: ~14 mg/g
- Odens Extreme: 22+ mg/g
- ZYN 6mg: ~6 mg/g; ZYN 12mg: ~12 mg/g

## Packaging

### Dosa / Tin
- Round plastic tin, ~54mm diameter
- **~20 portions per tin** (varies: mini=24, slim=24, large=18; loose=50g)
- Lid has a small compartment: the **bjudlock** ("offering lid") — for storing used portions discreetly
- "Bjudlocket" literally = "the offering lid"

### Stock / Rulle
- **10 tins = 1 stock** (also called "rulle" informally)
- Standard retail unit: cylindrical stack of 10 tins wrapped in plastic
- Price in Sweden: ~400–600 SEK per stock (varies by brand)

### Kartong / Crate
- **10 stocks = 1 kartong (crate) = 100 tins**
- Used for bulk/wholesale

## Science & Quality

**Moisture:** ~40–55% water (original portions). White portions ~25–35%. Higher moisture = faster release, more drip. Store in fridge; freeze for long-term.

**pH:** ~8–8.5 (alkaline). The alkaline pH keeps nicotine in freebase form, which crosses oral mucosa rapidly. This is why snus delivers nicotine efficiently without inhalation.

**GOTHIATEK standard:** Swedish Match's quality standard. Sets maximum limits for harmful compounds (TSNAs, benzo[a]pyrene, arsenic, etc.). Swedish snus has significantly lower TSNA levels than American smokeless tobacco.

**Nicotine absorption:** Peak plasma nicotine reached in ~15–30 minutes. More sustained than cigarettes (slower spike, longer plateau). No combustion = no CO, no smoke carcinogens.

## Regulations

- **Sweden**: Legal, age 18+. Regulated by the Swedish Food Agency (Livsmedelsverket). Snus is uniquely classified partly as a food product in Sweden.
- **EU**: Sale banned in all EU member states **except Sweden** (EU Tobacco Products Directive, Article 17). Sweden negotiated a permanent exemption when joining EU in 1995.
- **Norway / Iceland** (EEA, not EU): Legal to sell.
- **UK** (post-Brexit): Legal.
- **USA**: Legal. Swedish Match has sold General snus in the US. Subject to FDA regulation.
