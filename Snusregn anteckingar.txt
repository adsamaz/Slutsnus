1. The Perfect Moment (Timing Game)
A calm fishing scene where you wait for the fish to bite. When it does, you have a narrow window to both reel in the fish AND pop a snus at the exact right moment. Nail both = massive satisfaction score with screen effects. Miss the timing = the fish gets away or the snus falls in the water.

2. Patience & Reward (Idle/Clicker)
A slow-burn idle game where waiting is the core mechanic. Your fisherman sits still, building up a "tranquility meter." Catching a fish + taking a snus at peak tranquility triggers a euphoric combo that multiplies your score and unlocks new fishing spots or snus brands. The game rewards patience, not clicking.

3. The Buzz System (RPG/Progression)
Equip different snus types before each fishing session — they each give different buffs (e.g., "Göteborgs Rapé" = steady hands for precision casting, "General Strong" = adrenaline rush for faster reeling). Catching a rare fish while the right snus is active triggers a "fisksnus combo" with unique animations.

4. Post-Catch Ritual (Mini-game within a game)
After every successful catch, a short mini-game triggers: you have a few seconds to tap/swipe to perfectly tuck the snus in, rate the fish, and take a sip of coffee — all in sequence. Nailing the ritual builds a "streak" multiplier. Miss it and the vibe is broken.

5. Zen Fishing (Chill/Atmosphere)
No fail state. Just vibes. Cast, wait, catch, snus. But the game tracks your personal records and generates a "fishing diary" — your best sessions, your best catches, which snus you had that day. The goal is building a story, not a high score.
